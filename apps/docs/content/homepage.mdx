# Zerops Documentation

From local development to production. Zerops builds, deploys, runs and manages your apps, no matter the size, no matter the environment.

Regardless of the application size, whether it's a simple web interface or a complex system with numerous microservices; regardless of the traffic, from just a few visits daily to millions; and irrespective of the environment, be it production, staging, or even local and remote development, Zerops provides developers of contemporary software with a platform to develop, build, deploy, run, and manage their applications with ease, affordability, and confidence.
